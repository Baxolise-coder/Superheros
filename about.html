About us

The Avengers are a team of superheroes and the protaganists of the Marvel Cinamatic Universe media franchise, based on the eponymous team from Marvel Comics created by Stan Lee and Jack Kirby in 1963.

Founded by S.H.I.E.L.D. director Nick Fury, the team is United States based organization composed primarily of superpowered and gifted individuals, described as "Earth's Mightiest Heroes", who are committed to he world's protection from a variety of treats.
